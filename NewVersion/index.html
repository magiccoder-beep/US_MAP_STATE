ATTEND LIVE EVENTS: PERCENT OF U.S. ADULTS
WHO ATTEND VISUAL OR PERFORMING ARTS EVENTS

Includes: attending a fair or festival that featured crafts, visual arts, or performing artists such as musicians, singers, dancers, or actors; an art exhibit such as paintings, sculpture, pottery, photography, or digital art; a live music performance; a live play or musical; a live dance performance; an event featuring a poet or writer; other kind of live performance such as improv comedy or a circus performance

READ: PERCENT OF U.S. ADULTS WHO READ LITERATURE
(PLAYS, POETRY, SHORT STORIES, OR NOVELS)

Includes: reading novels, short stories, poems, or plays.

CREATE: PERCENT OF U.S. ADULTS WHO
PERSONALLY PERFORM OR CREATE ARTWORKS

Includes: making pottery, ceramics or jewelry; making leatherwork, metalwork, or woodwork; making weaving, crocheting, or other textile art; playing a musical instrument; acting; performing or practicing dance; performing or practicing singing; creating films or videos; taking photographs for artistic purposes; creating other visual arts (e.g., paintings, sculpture, or graphic design; and doing creative writing)

CONSUME DIGITAL MEDIA: PERCENT OF U.S. ADULTS WHO USE TV, RADIO,
AND/OR THE INTERNET TO CONSUME ART OR ARTS PROGRAMMING

Use TV, Radio, and/or the Internet to watch, listen to, and/or download any: jazz; Latin, Spanish, or salsa music; classical music; opera; rock, pop, country, folk, rap, or hip-hop; musicals or stage plays; performance or programs or information about dance; programs or information about visual arts; programs or information about books or writers.
